# Listen

A simple, open-source tool to hear the unique "heart song" of any number or text.

Enter a number, name, phrase, or poem — and listen to its continuous frequency evolution shaped by the golden ratio and binary structure.

## Vision
Exploring divine knowledge through pure mathematics and sonification. Each input creates its own dynamic, unique song — a sonic reflection of its geometry.

Built with love, for those who love to listen.

1. Run `app.py`

3. Hear the song
